# Interface: Rectangle

Ok, this is gonna sound weird, but all rectangles are squares. Also, we only permit side lengths
that are powers of two, and ALSO!! The side lengths must be between {@link MIN_CHUNK_SIZE} and
{@link MAX_CHUNK_SIZE}.
